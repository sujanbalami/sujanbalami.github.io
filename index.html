About me

"Dream it and do it"

Hello everyone, I am Sujan originally form Nepal. Currently, I am Ph.D. student at the Department of Botany, Faculty of Science, University of South Bohemia in Ceske Budejovice, Czech Republic. My research interest is in resoration ecology where I specifically focus on fungal domain.

Education

  • Ph.D. in Botany (ongoing) from University of South Bohemia in Ceske Budejovice, Czech Republic
  • M.Sc. in Botany from Central Department of Botany, Tribhuvan University, Kirtipur, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • B.Sc. in Botany from Amrit Campus, Institute of Science and Technology, Tribhuvan University, Thamel, Kathmandu, Nepal
  • I.Sc. in Biology from Amrit Campus, Institute of Science and Technology, Tribhuvan University, Thamel, Kathmandu, Nepal
